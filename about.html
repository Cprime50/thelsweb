What We Do:

As a specialised consulting firm, we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to cater to the unique needs of higher education institutions, research centres, scientific organizations, and innovative startups. Our areas of expertise include:

1. Higher Education Consulting: We work closely with universities and colleges to enhance their academic programs, optimise administrative processes, and elevate their global reputation. Our experts guide research, educational events, job posting, strategic planning, and student experience improvement.

2. Science and Research Support: Thels Impact Consulting Ltd is deeply rooted in the research community. We support researchers, laboratories, and research organisations in securing funding, managing projects efficiently, attracting collaboration with international institutions and ensuring compliance with ethical and regulatory standards. We aim to accelerate scientific breakthroughs and advancements.

3. Job Posting: Through our platform, we connect qualified individuals with exciting opportunities in the academic and research sectors. We bridge the gap between talent and institutions, fostering growth for both.

4. Conference and Event Promotion: We are a hub for academics, researchers, and professionals to discover and engage with conferences, workshops, and events. Our platform serves as a dynamic space for networking, learning, and collaboration.
