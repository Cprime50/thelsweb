Our Services

we are committed to making a tangible difference in the Nigerian Educational Landscape.

At Thels Impact Consulting

We are passionate about making a positive impact by addressing the challenges faced by Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) in Nigeria. Through our full-package initiative, we aim to bridge the gap between knowledge development and practical implementation, offering a range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of HEIs. With our expertise and dedication, we strive to elevate the standards of education and research in Nigeria.

Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E):

We understand the importance of tracking progress and ensuring that HEIs are on the right path towards achieving their goals. Our M&E solutions provide a comprehensive framework to assess and measure performance. By implementing suitable and effective systems, we help institutions optimize their operations and enhance their impact. Our M&E services include:

  • Progress tracking and reporting
  • Program and project evaluation
  • Performance metrics analysis
  • Educational Technology integration

Research and Training:

Research is at the core of knowledge development, and we take pride in our ability to push the frontiers of science. Our team of experts is equipped with a wealth of knowledge and experience, enabling us to provide top-notch research and training services to HEIs. Our Research and Training services encompass:

  • Cutting-edge research projects
  • Knowledge-base capacity building
  • Facilitating effective training sessions, seminars, and conferences
  • Event management services for HEIs

HigherEd Consulting:

We serve as trusted advisors to HEIs, offering comprehensive consulting services to enhance their operations and strategic planning. Our expertise enables us to conduct feasibility studies, perform SWOT analysis, and provide recommendations to foster growth and development. Our HigherEd Consulting includes:

  • Feasibility studies and strategic planning
  • SWOT analysis and strategy development
  • Equipment procurement and logistics support
  • Guidance on regulatory compliance and accreditation

At Thels Impact Consulting, we are committed to making a tangible difference in the Nigerian Educational Landscape. We believe that strong partnerships and collaborations with HEIs can unlock the potential for positive change and innovation in the education sector. Let us join forces and create a brighter future together.

For inquiries and to discuss how we can assist with your specific needs, please don't hesitate to contact us. We look forward to working with you and contributing to the growth and success of your institution. Together, we can make a lasting impact!
